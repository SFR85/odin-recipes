Summer Sausage Traybake

I usually think of sausage bakes as wintery dishes, but they work just as well with fresh courgettes, tomatoes and broccoli for a light, summery dinner.

Serves: 2 adults + 2 children.
Preparation time: 10 minutes.
Cooking time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250g Tenderstem or ordinary broccoli
  • 500g courgettes, cut into 5mm slices
  • 300g cherry vine tomatoes
  • 1 red onion finely sliced
  • 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 2-3 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • 1 tsp sea salt flakes (optional)
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes (optional - leave out for children)
  • 8-12 free-range chipolata sausages
  • 1 lemon, juice only

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 230C/210C fan/gas 8.
  2. Put the broccoli into a large bowl, pour over a kettleful of boiling water, leave to stand for 1 minute, then drain well. If using ordinary broccoli, skip this step.
  3. Tip everything into a roasting tin large enough to hold it all in a single layer, and mix well. Make sure the chipolata sausages are on top, then transfer to the oven and bake for 30 minutes.
  4. If your oven heats unevenly, turn the roasting tin after about 20 minutes so the sausages brown evenly.
  5. Squeeze a little lemon juice and sea salt over the vegetables to taste and serve hot.
